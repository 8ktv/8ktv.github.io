How We Got Here (The Slow Death)

Why The Hell Is This Happening?

Man... Rec Room hit over 150 million players. We had dorm parties, stupid inventions, Rec Royale chaos, and rooms that somehow became legends. And they're still shutting it down on June 1, 2026.

The devs basically said: "We never figured out how to make money without bleeding cash." Costs kept winning against the UGC payouts. They laid off half the team back in August 2025 trying to fix it, but it wasn't enough.

"Our costs always ended up overwhelming the revenue we brought in."
— Official blog (translation: we tried, but money said no)

Feels weird knowing all those late nights grinding tokens and building dumb shit are about to disappear. A lot of us basically grew up here.

They gave us some time at least — data downloads start in May so creators can try saving their rooms. But after June 1? Login gone. Rec.net offline. Everything just... poof.

Half the Discords are still in denial saying "April Fools right???"
